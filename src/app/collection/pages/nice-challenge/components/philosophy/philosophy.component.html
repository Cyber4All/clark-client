Hosted at California State University at San Bernardino (CSUSB), the NICE Challenge Project provides competency-based assessments focused on real-world problems and context, designed to capture useful data for actionable metrics.

The National Initiative for Cybersecurity Education (NICE) Challenge Project develops real-world cybersecurity challenges within virtualized business environments that bring students the experience before joining the workforce.
