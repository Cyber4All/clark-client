The CLARK Platform is developed and maintained by TU students and relies on government and industry funding for support.

How to Contribute

Individual contributions

You can support the CLARK effort to help solve the cybersecurity problem by providing free resources to faculty.

Corporate Giving Levels

Corporate logo will appear on the home page for all corporate giving levels

Bronze: $5,000

Silver: $10,000

Supports Annual cloud hosting services

Gold: $15,000

Supports a full tuition scholarship (tuition + stipend) for one graduate student on the CLARK team
