Related curricula grouped hierarchically

A learning object is not just a single lesson or lab and although they can often be used individually, it is often constructed of multiple labs and lessons. Hierarchies on CLARK are designed to provide a structure for combining individual learning objects into larger modules, units, or courses. Taking a modular approach to creating curriculum allows for educators to organize their materials in a way that supports the learning outcomes and allows easy use in the classroom.
