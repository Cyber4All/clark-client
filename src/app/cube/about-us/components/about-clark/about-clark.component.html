CLARK hosts the largest compilation of free, high-value, high-impact CYBERSECURITY curriculum. Educators looking to teach new cybersecurity COURSES or expand and improve their instruction can find ready-to-use modular content, including videos, labs, and slides that can be reused and modified under Creative Commons 4.0.

CLARK supports the model for BUILDING modularized curriculum, the digital library system to BROWSE free curriculum, and unique COLLECTIONS to categorize distinct groups of curriculum. CLARK provides cybersecurity educators with the tools needed to better prepare and equip the NEXT WAVE of cybersecurity professionals and researchers.

The CLARK system, hosted at www.clark.center, was created as a high-availability, relevant, and scalable repository for free curricular resources in the cybersecurity education community. With several thousand users and over 1000 modularized pieces of content, CLARK is establishing itself as the go-to source for free peer-reviewed cybersecurity curricula. The system is built based on lessons learned from research on digital libraries and learning sciences, making CLARK easy to use by both instructors wanting to share their curricula with the community and instructors needing ready-to-use lessons to teach their cyber classes tomorrow.
