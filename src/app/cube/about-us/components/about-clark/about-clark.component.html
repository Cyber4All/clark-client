The CLARK curriculum development model is an innovative process for building quality and maintaining relevance in cybersecurity curriculum. It involves developing outcome-based curriculum based on standard templates. The content creators work in tandem with instructional designers, and the curriculum undergoes reviews by subject matter experts and the technical editing team at CLARK.
