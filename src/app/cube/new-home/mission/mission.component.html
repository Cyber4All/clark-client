Securing the nation through education

CLARK, the Cybersecurity Labs and Resource Knowledge-base, is a platform for building and sharing free cybersecurity curricula. It includes a model for building cirriculum, the digital library system, and distinct curriculum collections.

CLARK fulfills a demonstrated need for a high-quality and high-availability repositor for curricular resources for the cybersecurity education community. It provides cybersecurity educators with the building blocks to train the next wave of researchers and better prepare the cybersecurity workforce.
